# Back button blues
## 2011/10/26 21:13

[John Gruber][1] on Android's back button...

> Being able to label the back button is a big reason why the iPhone's 
> on-screen buttons are better than Android's hardware Back button. A 
> dedicated hardware Back button can never answer the question "Where?"  
> -- [Labeling the back button][2]

Funny. I've been using an Android phone for more than a year now and 
 have never needed to ask that question. Does the back button in Safari 
and all other web browsers present similar problems for iPhone users? I 
doubt it.
